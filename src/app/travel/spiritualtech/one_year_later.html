In this series, I'll be sharing reflections on each day of my journey, exactly one year after they happened.

Since I wasn't able to share as much as I wanted to in the moment, I figured this might keep the road trip alive, and allow me to reflect and build on the experiences I had.

The delayed perspective offers a unique opportunity to see how time has shaped my understanding of the experiences, the people I met, and the lessons learned. Each entry will blend original notes with the clarity that comes from a year of reflection, capturing both the immediacy of the moment and the wisdom of hindsight. The format will be straightforward: a brief account of the day's events followed by my present-day thoughts, allowing the contrast between then and now to highlight the journey's deeper meaning.
